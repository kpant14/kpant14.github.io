Kartik Anand
Pant

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics at Purdue University, advised by Dr. Inseok Hwang and Dr. James Goppert. My research focuses on distributed and resilient control of multi-robot systems, with applications in UAV cybersecurity, coverage control, and safe autonomy.

Previously, I was a Scientist/Engineer at ISRO (VSSC) and a Graduate Research Intern at Toyota Research Institute of North America. I received my M.S. (4.0/4.0) and am completing my Ph.D. at Purdue, and hold a B.Tech. from IIT (BHU) Varanasi.
